About Me

I am a Graphic and Web Designer in Fresno, California. I am new to professional Graphic Design but have been enthusiastic about design since 2010 while attending Center for Advanced Research and Technology (CART) . I furthered my education in multimedia at DeVry University straight after high school and graduated in July 2015! While there, I worked with non-profits and in projects for businesses around California.
